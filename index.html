About Dr. Renge

Academic credibility meets practical industry leadership.

Dr. Mohan Renge is a senior professor and consultant specializing in microcontrollers, DSP-IC design, power electronics, and electric drives. With experience spanning leading universities, R&D labs, and industrial collaborations, he guides learners and engineering teams toward deployable solutions.

His portfolio includes mentoring postgraduate research, architecting embedded control for renewable and motor-drive applications, and advising manufacturers on digitally controlled power converters tailored to Indian and global standards.
