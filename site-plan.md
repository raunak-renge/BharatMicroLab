# Bharat Micro Lab Website Plan

## Audience & Voice
- Blend academic authority and hands-on industrial credibility.
- Address students, researchers, engineering teams, and SMEs seeking consulting.

## Page Structure
1. **Hero / Value Proposition**
   - Introduce Dr. Mohan Renge as mentor, researcher, consultant.
   - CTA buttons: "Explore Expertise", "WhatsApp Dr. Renge".
2. **About Dr. Renge**
   - Academic credentials, research publications, industrial track record.
   - Highlight dual focus: teaching + consulting.
3. **Areas of Expertise** (cards)
   - Embedded Microcontroller Systems
   - DSP & Control Algorithms
   - Renewable & Power Electronics
   - **Industrial Power-Electronics Design & Consulting** (new card per brief)
4. **Consulting & Advisory**
   - Real-world project support, industries served, sample deliverables.
5. **DSP-IC Lab / Project Mentoring**
   - Facilities, mentorship process, student + industry fit.
   - Bullet for industry-scale prototyping support and startups/SMEs.
6. **Courses & Workshops**
   - Flagship courses.
   - Optional card: Industry-Oriented Corporate Training & Workshop.
7. **Research & Publications**
   - Showcase focus areas, selected works.
8. **Success Stories / Testimonials**
   - Students, corporate clients.
9. **Engagement Process**
   - How mentoring or consulting collaboration works.
10. **Contact & WhatsApp**
    - Email, phone, WhatsApp CTA.
    - Explicit invitation for students + industry professionals.

## Design Notes
- Single-page responsive layout with sticky header.
- Warm academic palette (deep blue, saffron accent, neutral background).
- Use CSS grid/flex for cards; mobile-first adjustments.
- Smooth scrolling + subtle shadows for depth.
